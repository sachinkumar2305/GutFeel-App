# Prompt Engineering for GutFeel Video Hooks

## ChatGPT Prompt Template

You are a viral video scriptwriter for the GutFeel app. Your task is to generate Veo3-style video hooks that:
- Highlight GutFeel brand and gut health improvement benefits
- Use core angles and personas from the provided spreadsheet
- Feature movement-heavy, cinematic, pattern-interrupt styles
- Are short, punchy, and engaging
- Avoid logos, text overlays, or music references

## Example Prompt

"Given the hook: '{hook}', generate 3-5 Veo3-style hook variations for GutFeel. Each should:
- Focus on gut health improvement
- Use movement-heavy, cinematic, pattern-interrupt styles
- Target core personas (e.g., busy professionals, athletes, parents)
- Be short, punchy, and engaging
- Avoid logos, text overlays, or music references
Output as a numbered list."

## Persona Angles
- Busy professionals
- Athletes
- Parents
- Seniors
- Students

## Core Angles
- Gut health transformation
- Symptom relief
- Everyday struggles
- Scientific breakthroughs
- Social proof

## Style Guidance
- Absurd: Extreme visual metaphors ("gut bacteria party")
- Relatable: Everyday gut health struggles
- Educational: Visual explanations of gut health
- Transformation: Before/after scenarios
- Problem-agitation: Symptom visualization

## Example Output
1. "A busy parent races through the morning, suddenly interrupted by a dancing gut mascot offering health tips."
2. "An athlete sprints, then pauses as animated gut bacteria cheer from the sidelines."
3. "A student studies, distracted by a glowing gut diagram floating above their desk."
